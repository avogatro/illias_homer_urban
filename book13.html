Poseidon Makes a Move

While Zeus was chillin' on the mountain, Poseidon was watching the Greeks get wrecked.

He felt bad for them. He was like, "Nah, I can't let them die."

He snuck out of the ocean. He was careful not to let Zeus see him. Zeus was distracted, looking the other way.

Poseidon put on a disguise. He looked like a random human prophet.

The Pep Talk

Poseidon ran straight to the two Ajaxes (Big Ajax and Little Ajax).

"Yo, listen," he whispered. "If you guys wanna survive, you gotta toughen up. Hector thinks he's winning. You gotta shut him down."

He didn't just talk. He hit them with a magical energy boost. Suddenly, the Ajaxes felt unstoppable. They were ready to run through a wall.

Poseidon kept running down the line, hyping everyone up. "Do it for your ships! Do it for your lives!"

Idomeneus the Savage

Then Poseidon found Idomeneus, a Cretan captain.

"You're the man, Idomeneus. Where's your spear at?"

Idomeneus was hyped. "I'm ready."

He saw a Trojan friend named Meriones. "Yo, give me a spear. I left mine in the tent."

Meriones gave him a good bronze one. "Here. Don't miss."

The Rampage

Idomeneus started killing everyone. He was in the zone.

He dropped a Trojan named Othryoneus who just showed up trying to marry Cassandra.

"Nice try, bro," Idomeneus said. "You said you'd kick the Greeks out. Look at you now. Dead."

He killed like 5 guys in a row. He was showing off.

Deiphobus vs. Idomeneus

A Trojan prince named Deiphobus saw Idomeneus flexing.

He challenged him. "You think you're hard? Watch this."

He threw his spear. It hit Idomeneus' shield.

Idomeneus threw his back. *SHANK.* It went right into Deiphobus' gut.

Deiphobus was screaming in pain. His brother ran over to carry him away. It was a mess.

The Wall Holds

With Poseidon's magic buffing them, the Greeks pushed back hard.

The Trojans stopped laughing. They were getting pushed away from the ships.

Hector was still fighting, but his squad was crumbling. The wall was safe for now.

Poseidon kept cheering from the sidelines. "Keep pushing! Don't let them win!"
