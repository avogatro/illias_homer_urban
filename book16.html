The Begging

Patroclus couldn't watch anymore. The Greeks were getting cooked right in front of their ships.

He ran to Achilles, tears in his eyes. "Bro, look at them! They’re dying. If you won't fight, let me wear your armor. Maybe if the Trojans see *you*, they'll get scared."

Achilles looked at him. He was torn. He still hated Agamemnon, but he loved Patroclus.

"Fine," Achilles said. "Take my team. Take my gear. But listen close."

"Push them back from the ships. That's it. Do NOT chase them all the way to the city. If you get cocky, you might catch a stray. I need you alive."

The Glitch

Patroclus suited up. He looked exactly like Achilles. He put the Myrmidons in the chariots.

They stormed out of camp like a tidal wave.

The Trojans saw him and froze. "Oh snap! It's Achilles! He's back!"

The Trojans panicked. They thought the tank was back in the game. They started running immediately.

Patroclus was eating them up. He killed like 5 guys in the first minute.

Sarpedon Dies

Zeus watched his son, Sarpedon, fighting Patroclus.

Zeus wanted to save him. But Hera checked him. "If you save your son, all the other gods are gonna want to save their favorites. It'll break the game."

Zeus sighed. He rained bloody tears on the battlefield.

Patroclus threw his spear. *THUNK.* Right through Sarpedon's heart. The Lycian king fell.

His buddy Glaucus saw it and screamed. "Avenge the King!"

There was a huge fight over the body. But the Greeks grabbed Sarpedon's gear and dragged him away. Zeus sent Apollo to clean the body and take it back to Troy for a proper funeral.

The Pride (Hubris)

Patroclus was winning too much. He forgot the rules.

He killed the guy who stabbed Odysseus earlier. He killed Hector’s driver.

He kept pushing and pushing. He went past the wall. He went past the trench. He was right at the gates of Troy.

He was trying to storm the castle alone. Apollo saw this and was not impressed.

The Check

Apollo walked up behind Patroclus. "Sit down, son," he said.

He slapped Patroclus' back so hard his helmet spun around. His shield fell off. He was stunned.

A random Trojan named Euphorbus saw his chance. He threw a spear and stuck Patroclus in the back.

Hector rode up on his chariot. He saw the great warrior down.

Hector shouted, "You thought you could sack Troy? Watch this."

He stabbed Patroclus right in the gut with a spear.

Patroclus looked him in the eye. "You got me, but you didn't kill a random. I was wearing Achilles' armor. And Hector? You're next. You're days are numbered."

Patroclus died.

The war had just gotten personal.
