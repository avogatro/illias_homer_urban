The God Summit

Zeus looked down from Olympus. He saw Achilles in that new shiny armor.

He called the gods. "Listen up," he said. "I know you all want to jump in. Today is the day."

"Side with whoever you want. Help the Greeks or the Trojans. But if I see any god trying to play both sides, I'm throwing them into the dark pit."

The gods rushed down. It was a full-on street fight now.

Team Greek vs Team Troy

The gods picked their squads.

On the Trojan side: Ares, Apollo, Artemis, Leto, Xanthus (the river god).

On the Greek side: Hera, Athena, Poseidon, Hermes, and Hephaestus.

They stood on the hill overlooking the battlefield, shaking the earth just by standing there.

Achilles Enters the Chat

Achilles stepped out. His armor looked like fire.

He wasn't just fighting anymore. He was looking for Hector specifically.

Aeneas, a Trojan hero, saw him. He stepped up to the plate.

"Let's see if you're all talk," Aeneas said.

"Aeneas?" Achilles said. "You think you can take me? Last time you ran from me."

Aeneas dropped some history on him. "My mom is Aphrodite. I'm not scared."

The Fight

They threw spears. They threw rocks. It was intense.

Achilles was about to spear Aeneas in the throat. But Poseidon was watching. He loves Aeneas because he's a descendant of his own kid.

Poseidon threw a magical mist. He swooped in, lifted Aeneas up, and threw him out of the danger zone.

"You're dumb," Poseidon said to Aeneas. "Why fight a demigod? Go find some weaker soldiers."

Aeneas landed back in his squad.

Rampage Begins

Achilles was furious his kill got stolen. He started killing everyone else.

"You die. And you. You too."

He saw Hector's brother, Polydorus, trying to run.

Achilles threw a spear and stabbed him right in the back. Polydorus fell with his guts hanging out.

Hector saw this. That was his little brother.

Hector got hype. He rushed Achilles, even though Apollo was whispering, "Bro, don't do it."

The Chase

Hector threw his spear. It hit Achilles' shield perfectly, but the shield was Hephaestus-made. It didn't break.

Achilles threw his back. It hit Hector's helmet but glanced off.

Achilles cursed the gods for saving Hector.

Hector turned to run. But Achilles was fast. He chased him.

Most of the Trojans bailed. They ran into the river Xanthus to hide in the water.

Achilles followed them into the river. He didn't care. He was going to kill them all.
