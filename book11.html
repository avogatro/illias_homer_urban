The Iliad

Urban Slang Edition • Book 11

Agamemmon's Moment

Eos (the Dawn) showed up. Zeus woke up and sent Iris to deliver a message.

"Hey Agamemnon," Iris whispered. "Get dressed. Zeus wants you to hype your boys up. You're gonna win big today."

Agamemnon put on his gear. He looked fresh. Massive armor, huge shield, sword like a chopping block.

The soldiers were hyped. They marched out like a swarm of bees.

The Rampage

Agamemnon was on one today. He was killing everything that moved.

Trojan soldiers were scattering. He was chopping them down like a man cutting grass. It was ugly.

He killed two of Priam’s sons. He killed their charioteers. Blood and dust everywhere.

The Trojans were folding. They were running back to the city gates.

The L

Just as Agamemnon was chasing them, a Trojan named Coon stepped up.

His brother was just killed by Agamemnon. Coon was mad.

He snuck up and speared Agamemnon right in the arm. It wasn't a fatal hit, but it hurt bad. He missed the elbow but went through the muscle.

Agamemnon was shook. He told his charioteer, "Turn this car around. I need to patch this up."

He dipped from the battlefield. When Hector saw the boss leave, he got hype. "The king is down! Let's push!"

Diomedes Out

Hector charged into the Greek lines. Diomedes tried to stop him.

But remember Paris? The guy who started all this? He was hiding behind a rock with his bow.

He shot Diomedes in the foot. Right in the toes.

Diomedes cursed at him. "You pretty boy shooter! You aim at the feet because you're scared of the face?"

He grabbed the arrow, pulled it out, and told his driver to bail. He hopped back to the ships.

Odysseus vs. The Squad

Odysseus was left alone. He looked at his squad. Everyone was retreating.

"What am I, a turtle? I don't run!" he said.

He stood his ground. He killed a Trojan leader. But then, he got surrounded.

It was 5 on 1. Then 10 on 1.

Socus, a Trojan, ran up and speared Odysseus in the ribs. *Shank.* It went deep. Odysseus got dizzy.

But he threw his spear back and killed Socus. He was bleeding out. He yelled, "Yo, Ajax! Help me!"

The Save

Ajax the Great heard the scream. He came running like a tank.

He charged into the crowd. Trojans scattered like bugs.

Menelaus came too. They grabbed Odysseus and dragged him to safety.

Odysseus was bleeding all over the chariot. Ajax kept holding the Trojans back while they got away.

The Doctor Down

Things were looking bad for the Greeks. The Trojans were wilding.

Achilles (who was still chilling on his boat) saw Nestor driving a wounded soldier to the back.

It was Machaon, the doctor. He took an arrow in the shoulder.

Achilles turned to his best friend Patroclus. "Yo, go ask Nestor who that is. Why are they rushing the medic?"

Patroclus ran off. Little did he know, asking that question was gonna change his life forever.
