Hera's Scheme

Hera saw Poseidon helping the Greeks. She wanted to help him mess with Zeus.

She looked at Zeus and saw he was checking her out. She had a plan.

"I'm gonna distract him with the booty," she thought.

She went to Aphrodite, the goddess of love.

"Hey, girl," Hera lied. "I need to go visit Ocean and Tethys. They are fighting. Let me borrow that magic bra. The one that makes you irresistible."

Aphrodite was suspicious, but she didn't say no. "Here. Take it."

The Bribed Nap

Next, Hera found Hypnos, the god of Sleep.

"Listen, I need you to knock Zeus out tonight. I'll pay you big time," she said.

Hypnos was shaking. "No way. Last time I did that, he threw me into the ocean."

"I'll give you Pasithea," Hera offered. "She's the prettiest of the Graces. You can marry her."

Hypnos was down. "Bet. I’m in."

The Hookup

Hera put on the sexy gear. She smelled amazing. She walked up to Zeus on the mountain.

Zeus took one look and lost it. "Dayum," he said. "You look better than ever."

"Come on," Hera said, acting innocent. "Let's go find a spot."

Zeus was simping hard. "If we go there, I don't think I can control myself."

The Knockout

The Comeback
